Our Story

Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has evolved into one of the leading technology conferences in Africa. What began as a small gathering of local innovators has now grown into a global event attracting thousands of attendees, hundreds of speakers, and dozens of technology partners every year.

TechCon’s mission is to inspire, educate, and connect people who are passionate about technology and its power to transform the world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we believe technology should be inclusive, sustainable, and human-centered. Our mission is to provide a platform where industry experts, startups, and students can exchange ideas and showcase innovations that shape the future.
